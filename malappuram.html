Malappuram

Malappuram is known for its natural beauty and for having a historical past. It is also known for its temples and Moplah mosques.

Some of the tourist spots are Thirunavaya, Thali Temple, Thirumandhamkunnu Temple, The Jama-at Mosque, Pazhayangadi Mosque, Nilambur, Tirur, Tanur, Kottakkal, Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary, Kottappadi, Padinharakara Beach, Vallikunnu Beach, Kadampuzha, Trikandiyur Shiva Temple, Biyyan Kayal,Kodikuthimala, Adyanpara etc:
