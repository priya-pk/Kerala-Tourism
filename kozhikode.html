Kozhikode

Kozhikode is endowed with lush green countrysides, serene beaches, historic sites, wildlife sanctuaries, rivers, and hills.

Some of the tourist spots are :Pazhassiraja Museum and Art Gallery, Krishna Menon Museum, Kalipoika, Kallai, S M Street, Kirtads, Beypore, Kappad, Kozhikode Beach, Velliyamkallu, Thikkoti Lighthouse, Payyoli Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary, Planetarium, Regional Science Centre, Kakkayam, Vadakara, Tusharagiri, Peruvannamuzhi, Vellari Mala, Wayanad Ghats,Kuttiyadi, Iringal, Mananchira Maidan, Kuttichira, Mishkal Masjid, Pishakarikavu, Lokanarkavu Temple, Tali Temple,Mannur Temple, Ponmeri, Varakkal Devi Temple, Jain Temple, Parsi Anju Amman Baug, Buddha Vihar, Muchunthi Palli, Mother of God Church, St. Mary’s Church etc…
