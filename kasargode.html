Kasaragod

The northernmost district of Kerala, Kasaragod is world renowned for its coir and handloom industries. Fishing is a prime source of livelihood. Kasaragod is known as the land of gods, forts, rivers, hills and beautiful beaches. The fort at Bekal is the largest and best preserved in the State.

Some of the tourist spots are : Bekal, the Bekal Tourism Project, Kappil Beach, Anandasram, Ananthapura Lake Temple, Valiyaparamba, Chandragiri, Manjeswaram, Kanwatheertha Beach, Kanhangad Fort, Madiyankulam Durga Temple, Sree Mahalingeswara Temple, Kottancherry, Ranipuram, Trikkaripur, Madhur Temple, Edneer Mutt, Possadigumpe, Tulur Vanam, Pandiyan Kallu, Central Plantation Crop & Research Institute, Malik Deenar Mosque, Nileswaram etc
