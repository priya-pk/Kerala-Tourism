Ernakulam

Ernakulam district includes Kochi city, which is known as the commercial capital of Kerala. The district is famous for its ancient temples, churches, and mosques.

Some of the tourist spots are : Marine drive Cochin, Fort Cochin, Mattanchery, Old Harbor House, Koder House, Santa Cruz Basilica, St. Francis Church, Dutch Palace, VOC Gate, The United Club, The Bishop’s House, Fort Immanuel, The Dutch Cemetery, Bolghatty Island, Willingdon Island, Thakur House, David Hall, The Cochin Club, Cherai Beach, The Hill Palace Museum, Vamanamoorthy Temple, Santhanagopala-Krishnaswamy Temple, Kaladi, Kodanad elephant camp, Chendamangalam, Malayatoor, Bhoothathankettu, Thattekkadu Bird Sanctuary etc…
