Thrissur

Thrissur is known as the cultural capital of Kerala, and the land of Poorams. The district is famous for its ancient temples, churches, and mosques. Thrissur Pooram is the most colourful and spectacular temple festival in Kerala.

Some of the tourist spots are :Vadakkumnathan Temple, Archaeological Museum, Zoo, Art Museum, Town Hall, Aquarium, Vilangankunnu, Arattupuzha, Peechi dam, Guruvayoor, Cheruthuruthy, Athirapally, Vazhachal, Shakthan Thampuran Palace etc
